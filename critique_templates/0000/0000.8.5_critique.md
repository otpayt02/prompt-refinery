## Critique Template

### Goal Fit
- Does the canonical prompt aim at the actual end goal?
- What outcome is missing or overemphasized?

### Practicality
- Are the constraints realistic for time, cost, and tools?
- What should be simpler?

### Prompt Quality
- Is the role right?
- Are any important context, files, skills, or agent instructions missing?

### Acceptance
- Can this prompt be accepted verbatim?
- If not, paste exact edits.
